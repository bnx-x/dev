Setup Identification

What is an FVG (Fair Value Gap)?

An FVG is a price gap left behind when market moves quickly. It appears when there's no overlap between candle 1's high/low and candle 3's high/low, with candle 2 in between creating the gap.

BULLISH FVG: Gap between candle 1's HIGH and candle 3's LOW
Look for when: Market drops fast, leaves gap, price comes back to fill it

BEARISH FVG: Gap between candle 1's LOW and candle 3's HIGH
Look for when: Market rallies fast, leaves gap, price comes back to fill it
